about our POVERTY-FIGHTING CHARITY ORGANIZATION

What is CCF all about?

Like the world-changers who support our organization, we are focused on making a long-term impact by helping kids living in poverty.

We have a bold vision: graduating healthy, educated, empowered and employed young adults from our program so they can achieve the goal of breaking the cycle of poverty.
